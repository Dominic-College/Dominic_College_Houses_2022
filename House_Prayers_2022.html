House Prayers

The House Prayers were created in 2019 to better reflect the spirit of each house. They are commonly used in house assemblies and house events.

Bosco

Heavenly Father,
As members of the Bosco House family,
We strive to follow in the pathway of our saint, John Bosco
To follow his teahings of love an faith,
And to see us all as unique indivuals, that are created euqally.
Give us courage to follow the examples set by St John Bosco,
To be holy, compassionate and to love God.
To accept all responsibilities and show kindness,
To be a friend to all and inspire others.
We are blessed to be in this community,
We pray that we model Salesian values,
And be responsible, respectful, honest leaners,
As we play, learn and love one another.

St John Bosco,
Pray for us.

Guzman

Heavenly Father,
We gather as members of the Guzman House,
To celebrate and remember our patron saint, Dominic Guzman.
In this house we strive to always follow in his footsteps,
And to follow your teachings of knowledge and compassion.
As part of Guzman house we gather here as five different pastoral groups, as one whole collective, as one family.
May we share your guidance and your teachings and use these throughout our lives,
And know that we are all created and treated equally.
We are blessed to be in this community together
In the company of each other.
With your guidance we can follow the virtues of Dominic College,
Of being responsible, respectful, honest learners.
As we play, learn and love one another.

St Dominic Guzman,
Pray for us.

Savio

Heavenly Father,
We are a blessed family, a Savio family,
We hope to bring laughter and joy into the lives of others,
To bring happiness to all we meet.
Help us to bring friendship and understanding to everyone,
To be someone who can listen to others and understand.
WE thank you for the opportunity to connect with others,
And thank you for the joy it brings us,
As we work towardsmaking the world in your image,
And keeping it a sacred place for all.
Help us to take the teachings of St Dominic Savio into our hearts,
As we learn to be undertsanding and kind to our peers,
May you encourage us to look after others and always be loyal to you.

St Dominic Savio,
Pray for us.

Siena

Heavenly Father,
We are gathered here as one Siena family,
To spread love and wisdom amoung our house and our peers,
We ask the Holy Spirit to give us courage and strength as we walk the journey of life.
We pray for healing and comfort to be recieved by those who need it most,
And for all to have joy and peace.
Help us to lead and support all those in our College Community,
Let us aspireto be wise and courageiys like St Catherine of Siena,
to be charitable and kind-hearted,
May we bring peace to all we encounter and lead by example to make the world a better place.

St Catherine of Siena,
Pray for us.
